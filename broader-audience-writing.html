Policy Writing

Do online marketplaces reduce gender discrimination?

VoxDev · 2026

Examines how gender discrimination manifests in Pakistan's online marketplaces, finding that while digital platforms eliminate price gaps, women face disproportionate non-economic costs through unsolicited contact that may constrain their market participation.

Pakistan's Tax Problem: Not a Mystery But a Method

Insights for Change · 2026

Argues that Pakistan's tax challenges stem not from an elusive informal economy but from distortionary policies and political protection of undertaxed sectors — and proposes treating tax reform as an applied science grounded in evidence and behavioral insights.

Potential of Behavioral Insights to Improve Tax Compliance

Insights for Change · 2023

Explores how behavioral tools — social norms, simplification, and citizen engagement — can complement traditional enforcement to strengthen tax compliance in Pakistan.

The Dark Side of Real Estate: Uncovering its Role in Undermining Investment, Inequality, and Growth

CDPR Policy Blog · 2023

Examines how distortions in real estate markets — speculation, tax treatment, and informality — affect investment, inequality, and long-run growth.

Rationalizing Tax Expenditures

CDPR Policy Blog · 2023

Discusses the scale and structure of tax expenditures and the trade-offs involved in reforming exemptions and special regimes to broaden the tax base.

Electronic Invoice Monitoring Systems to Improve Tax Compliance

International Growth Centre (IGC) · 2022

Summarizes evidence on how e-invoicing and electronic monitoring can enhance VAT compliance and strengthen enforcement capacity in low- and middle-income settings.

Role of Supply Chains in Determining Farmers' Productivity and Consumer Prices

Mahbub ul Haq Research Center · 2021

Discusses how intermediaries, infrastructure, and information flows in agricultural supply chains shape farmer productivity and price transmission to consumers.
