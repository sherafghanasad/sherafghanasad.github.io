Engagements

Tax, Revenue & Formalization

Property Tax Reform — Punjab's Shift to Capital Valuation

2025 · Completed

Supported Punjab's transition of the Urban Immovable Property Tax (UIPT) from an annual-rental-value (ARV) basis to a capital-valuation (CV) system. Delivered technical training to ETNCD senior and middle management on assessment under the new system and on managing the transition. The CV system has since been rolled out province-wide and now underpins assessments; findings from the workshop series informed revisions to several UIPT administration rules.

Inter-Agency Coordination for Property Tax (UIPT)

Participatory Local Governance Programme (EU / BMZ) · 2024–present · Ongoing

Authored the study and led the 2024 workshop on improving coordination, cooperation, and communication across the agencies that administer Punjab's Urban Immovable Property Tax — ETNCD, the Finance Department, the Board of Revenue / Punjab Land Records Authority, municipal corporations, and the Urban Unit. The findings made the case for a standing coordination mechanism, which the Government of Punjab formally notified as the UIPT Coordination Working Group in 2025 — which I now support as technical resource person, setting its agenda and framing the challenges it takes up.

Informality & Tax Evasion: Wholesale-Retail & Real Estate

2023 (published 2025)

Co-authored a Pakistan Business Council–commissioned study quantifying tax evasion and informality across Pakistan's wholesale-retail and real-estate sectors and mapping the political economy that sustains them. Set out concrete reform pathways — data integration around a single personal identifier and point-of-sale systems for retail; valuation, capital-gains and transparency reform for real estate. The recommendations closely track the documentation reforms the federal government has since pursued in both sectors.

Public Finance & Distribution

World Bank — Sri Lanka: Distributional & Fiscal Analysis

Poverty & Equity Global Practice · Short-Term Consultant · Completed

Served as a short-term consultant to the World Bank's Poverty & Equity practice during Sri Lanka's post-crisis period, producing background distributional analysis for two of the Bank's flagship Sri Lanka reports: a CEQ (Commitment to Equity) assessment of how taxes and transfers fall across the income distribution under fiscal consolidation (supporting the Public Finance Review), and a household-level analysis of how heat stress, flooding, and crop-productivity shocks raise poverty across districts (feeding the Country Climate and Development Report).

Trade & Competitiveness

Reviving Punjab's Dry Ports — Assessing the Infrastructure Cess

with Pakistan Customs & port authorities · 2023 · Completed

Led an econometric study for the Punjab Revenue Authority on whether the Punjab Infrastructure Development Cess (PIDC) was driving the decline of the province's dry ports. The analysis found the PIDC had a negligible effect on import value, goods declarations, and the number of importing firms — pointing instead to import-clearance delays, the uncertain payoff of "speed money," and high freight charges (relative to Karachi) as the significant constraints. Combined econometric evidence with focus groups and stakeholder surveys; the findings bear directly on the current provincial debate over scrapping the cess.

Agriculture & Food Export Potential of Pakistan

2025

Lead-authored a study for the FCDO-funded Revenue Mobilisation, Investment and Trade (ReMIT) programme quantifying the unrealized export potential of ten priority agriculture and food products — from rice and bovine meat to cherries and olive oil — using trade-data analysis and stakeholder consultations. Diagnosed the cross-cutting constraints holding exports back (SPS / quality standards, post-harvest handling, trade facilitation), benchmarked Pakistan against India, Bangladesh, and Türkiye, and set out a product-level reform agenda across production, market access, and export facilitation.
