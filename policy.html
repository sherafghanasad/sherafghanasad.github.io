Policy

I work with governments and international organizations to turn economic evidence into better fiscal policy — across tax reform, local public finance, fiscal incidence, and trade. My approach pairs rigorous empirical analysis with direct engagement with the policymakers who act on it.

Focus Areas Tax Policy Local Public Finance Fiscal Incidence Trade & Competitiveness Climate & Distribution
Partners World Bank German Development Cooperation (GIZ) FCDO (UK) International Growth Centre Tax Authorities Ministry of Commerce

Engagements

Tax & Revenue Mobilization

Property Tax Reform

German Development Cooperation (GIZ) & provincial tax authority

Advising on transition from rental-value assessments to market-value systems. Activities include facilitating technical sessions with officials and field staff, emphasizing transparency, improved valuation practices, and enhanced local revenue performance.

Taxation & Trade Logistics

Provincial revenue authority

Analysis of how tax rules influence firms' import declarations and port-choice decisions. Mapped interactions between fiscal incentives and port capacity. Delivered recommendations for reducing logistics and trade facilitation distortions.

Market Structure & Informality

Pakistan Business Council & policy research consortium

Examined incentives and bottlenecks limiting major sectors from formal tax participation. Connected documentation practices to regulatory and tax system design. Informed policy dialogue on formalization and compliance strategies.

Public Finance & Fiscal Incidence

Fiscal Incidence

World Bank — Poverty & Equity GP

Employed CEQ framework to assess distributional effects of taxes and transfers. Evaluated progressivity amid fiscal consolidation following Sri Lanka's debt crisis. Identified reform options for strengthening equity and revenue sustainability.

Local Public Finance

German Development Cooperation (GIZ)

Leading interagency working group spanning tax authorities, municipalities, and planning agencies. Supporting integration of land, cadastral, and taxpayer data for improved valuation. Exploring tax-service linkages to strengthen accountability and local revenue performance.

Trade, Climate & Distribution

Trade & Global Value Chains

FCDO & Ministry of Commerce

Assessed export potential of high-value food products using product and destination-level data. Identified quality, standards, and compliance constraints limiting market access. Outlined actionable steps for deepening regional and global value chain participation.

Climate & Distribution

World Bank — Poverty & Equity GP

Estimated climate-risk exposure across households and regions. Linked vulnerability to labor income, employment, and poverty transitions. Contributed to World Bank's Country Climate and Development Report for Sri Lanka.
